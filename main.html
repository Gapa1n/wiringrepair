Client supplies

What you can provide to speed up diagnosis and reduce turnaround time.

Faster quote · Better accuracy

Send these (recommended)

  • Vehicle type: car / motorcycle / boat
  • Brand + model + year (or engine/ECU details if relevant)
  • Symptoms: intermittent, no-start, sensor error, lighting issue, charging issue
  • Photos: connector close-up + loom area + wide shot for routing context
  • Any history: water ingress, accident repair, modifications, recent maintenance

If you can supply the harness

  • Ship the harness (or bring it in) with notes describing the issue
  • Mark suspect areas with tape (if known)
  • Include connectors and mating parts if available
  • Packaging: protect connectors and avoid tight bends
Pro tip
A short video of the symptom (dashboard error, no-start, cut-out) can help.

FAQ

Answer objections before they block a sale.

Do you repair entire harnesses or only sections?

Most jobs are targeted repairs or partial rebuilds. Full replacement is rarely required.

Can I ship a harness to you?

Yes—workshop repairs can be handled via shipping. Contact us for packing guidance.

How do you validate a repair?

We perform continuity checks and practical validation where applicable, then provide short handover notes.

Do you work with workshops?

Yes. We can align on a repeatable process for diagnosis, repair scope, and documentation.
